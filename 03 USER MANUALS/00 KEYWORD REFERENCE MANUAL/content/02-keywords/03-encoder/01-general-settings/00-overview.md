# General settings

**Overview:**

This section covers all the setting and status variables related to encoder and other positional feedback sensors. Some Agito products support up to two encoders per axis. The feedback keywords for auxiliary encoder will have additional prefix “Aux” on top of counterpart keywords of main encoder, except for selected keywords such as AuxSinCosSet and AuxSinCosSig. From here on, description regarding the main encoder also applies to the auxiliary encoder.
